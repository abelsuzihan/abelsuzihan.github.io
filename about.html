Hello!

I'm Abel Su (Su Zihan), a designer and team builder with 15+ years of experience across brand identity, UX, and product design. I've worked with major platforms like CCTV, Tudou, LeEco, Renren, RTW, and Cider, helping shape products and brands for millions of users worldwide.

Outside of work, I enjoy illustration and exploring new creative outlets. My passion for hip-hop culture also led me to start OURDEN, one of China’s earliest online hip-hop communities, which became a space for music lovers and creators to connect and share ideas.

To me, design is about solving real problems and creating meaningful connections between people and technology. I love learning new tools, trying fresh ideas, and pushing creative boundaries.
